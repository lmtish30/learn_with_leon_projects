Why Khan Academy works

Personalized learning

Students practice at their own pace, first filling in gaps in their understanding and then accerlerating their learning.

Trusted content

Created by experts, Khan Academy's library of trusted, standards-aligned practice and lessons cover math K-12 through early college, grammar, science, history, AP, SAT, and more. It's all free for learners and teachers.

Tools to empower teachers

With Khan Academy, teachers can identify gaps in their students' understanding, tailor instructions, and meet the needs of every student.
